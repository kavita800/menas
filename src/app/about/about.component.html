Everyone walks into a salon hoping to be transformed, you want your stylist to look at you and create the best style for you. At Mena's Hair Design We believe your hair should not only compliment your lifestyle, it should be indicative of your personal style. From contemporary hair designs, pin ups to classic coifs, Mena's Hair Design stylist have an eye for cutting edge beauty. We promote healthy, vibrant looking hair. I opened my first store in 2002, and just opened my 8's store in Oct. 2018 (click on "locations" to find the closest store to you). We specialize in blow drying natural and relaxed hair. Wraps, hair extensions, roller and straw sets, custom coloring, highlights and cuts. Come in, relax on our cozy couch and watch as your experienced stylist creates your one of a kind beauty. Come and join a lot of my clients who been with me since 1996.
